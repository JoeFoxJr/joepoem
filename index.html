technology

strangers on a train

none of us make eye contact

no one says hello


we could become friends

but we all have smart phones

goodbye forever

the Times Square Ball is thoroughly unenthusiastic

if I'm late I'm late

if I'm early I'll wait

if I arrive right on time

then we'll all celebrate

this is the end
